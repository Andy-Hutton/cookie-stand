Pat's Cookies

Pat has come up with a idea, by combining his love for sweets and his passion for healthy ecosystems he has developed a recipe for a coffee-time confection. These are called Salmon Cookies, they are made into the shape of a salmon and are suitable for both humans and salmon to enjoy.

We've gone global! locations in Seattle, Tokyo, Dubai, Paris, Lima... Some-fin-fishy is going on!
