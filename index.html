Salmon Cookies

Pat's Cookies

Pat has come up with a idea, by combining his love for sweets and his passion for healthy ecosystems he has developed a recipe for a coffee-time confection.
These are called Salmon Cookies, they are made into the shape of a salmon and are suitable for both humans and salmon to enjoy.

Our Mission Statment

Pat set out with just a couple of sites about 50 years ago, to now having more than 200 fish farms operateing in Scotland, producing more than 150,000 tonnes of salmon a year.
Farmed salmon are usually just fed on processed feed yet our aim to please not just your pallet, BUT we want the salmon to enjoy the cookies too!

Cookie Recipes

Although our secret formula is under lock and key, we dont mind sharing our DIY recipe!

Ingridients

  • 80g granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 225g plain flour

Method

  1. Warm up oven to 180
  2. Mix all Ingridients together
  3. Layout mixture and shape using our cutter available here
  4. Put in oven for 10mins
  5. DO NOT BURN!
  6. Leave to cool and enjoy
