Salmon Cookies

Pat's Cookies

Pat has come up with a idea, by combining his love for sweets and his passion for healthy ecosystems he has developed a recipe for a coffee-time confection. These are called Salmon Cookies, they are made into the shape of a salmon and are suitable for both humans and salmon to enjoy.

Our Mission Statment

Pat set out with just a couple of sites about 50 years ago, to now having more than 200 fish farms operateing in Scotland, producing more than 150,000 tonnes of salmon a year. Farmed salmon are usually just fed on processed feed yet our aim to please not just your pallet, BUT we want the salmon to enjoy the cookies too!
